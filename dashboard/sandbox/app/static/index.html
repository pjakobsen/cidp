About

Welcome to the Canadian International Development Platform (CIDP). The CIDP is a one-of-a-kind data and analysis driven interactive platform on Canada’s engagement with the developing world. The aim of the CIDP is to engage Canadians on international development issues and elevate the quality of debate by grounding discussions in the best available evidence. The CIDP is hosted by The North-South Institute (NSI), Canada’s only independent international development think-tank. NSI was recently ranked No. 1 small think-tank in the world by the Global Go-To Think Tanks Index - See more at: http://cidpnsi.ca/home/about/#sthash.OpRooI1q.dpuf.
